Review Policy

I. REVIEW AND PRIVACY POLICY Trusted Business Reviews is committed to providing the most useful reviews and protecting your privacy.  To demonstrate our commitment we have disclosed our review and privacy policy below.  In this policy, “we”, “us”, “our” and other similar references mean Trusted Business Reviews and “you” and “your” and other similar references mean a specific user of the Trusted Business Reviews website. If you have questions or suggestions regarding our review and privacy policy or our review and privacy practices, please contact us at info@TrustedBusinessReviews.com. II. PERSONAL INFORMATION Trusted Business Reviews does not collect any personal information from our website users.  Such personal information includes any information that identifies you, would enable someone to contact you, or that describes your relationship with Trusted Business Reviews.  Users may browse our website anonymously. III. VOLUNTARY SUBMISSION OF A REVIEW Trusted Business Reviews does not require the submission of personal information in order to publish a company or business review or access the website. Users may browse our website anonymously. If your name is entered as part of a review submission process, only your first name will be published on the website unless given written permission by the reviewer. Your email will remain private and will not be published, rented, sold or traded. Reviews submitted by a visitor that contain personal information will not be published. If Trusted Business Reviews elects to collect personal information in the future, we will do so in compliance with the GBLA directive.  Notice of any such future changes will be given in accordance with Section VIII of this Privacy Policy. IV. REVIEW AUTHENTICATION As an independent and trusted review agent who advocates for the both the consumer and the business owner, Trusted Business Reviews guarantees: - Published reviews are never altered - Our Review Acceptance and Publishing Policy is strictly applied to all reviews that are written for each business Under the policy, non-compliant reviews may not be displayed at the discretion of Trusted Business Reviews. Reviews that are published on a Trusted Business Reviews web page are written by customers of the business being reviewed. To maintain their privacy, we do not publish the last name of the reviewer unless the reviewer writes or approves the listing of their last name in the main body of the review. V. REVIEW ACCEPTANCE AND PUBLISHING POLICY Trusted Business Reviews will only accept and publish reviews that are submitted by authentic consumers of our business member products or services. In most cases, reviews will be published without contest, however, if the review meets one of the criteria below, it will be flagged and potentially removed at the sole discretion of Trusted Business Reviews. Reviews may be quarantined for further investigation or removed if: - The review cannot be verified as being posted by a customer of the business product or service. Verification may be performed by contacting both the user who submitted the review and the business owner. - The review contains profanity or considered offensive to a visitor of the Trusted Business Reviews website. - The review is not useful to a visitor of Trusted Business Reviews in making a determination regarding the capability or performance of a business member to deliver their product or service. - The review violates the privacy of a person or business. - The review is duplicated on another website. VI. TERMINATION OF A BUSINESS LISTING Business listings displayed on Trusted Business Reviews are published when a business member subscribes to the listing service. In a case where the subscription expires or is terminated by either party, both the business and reviews will be removed from Trusted Local Reviews. This may result in the removal of the business information and reviews from other third party websites and search engine results. VII. X. LANGUAGE The language of this Review Policy is English.  No translation, if any, of this Review Policy will be of any effect in the interpretation of this Review Policy or in determining the intent of Trusted Business Reviews. CHANGES TO THIS REVIEW AND PRIVACY POLICY Trusted Business Reviews may at our sole discretion modify this review and privacy policy at anytime without notice.  If any material changes are made, we will notify you by displaying a prominent announcement above the text of this review policy for thirty (30) days.  If you continue to use our website following the posting of changes to this review and privacy policy, you agree to be bound by the changes.
