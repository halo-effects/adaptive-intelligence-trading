Universal Glass

Universal Glass strives to be the best, not the biggest, glass replacement company in Phoenix. As a local family owned auto glass and window repair contractor, the company prides itself in building long term relationships with local residential, commercial and automotive clients. We have generations of customers that return for our superior service and impeccable workmanship. We take on projects others glass companies won't. From high end luxury sedans and sports cars like Rolls Royce, Lamborghini and Ferrari, to antique wooden windows in the historic district of downtown Phoenix, we are confident in our auto glass technicians and glaziers to perform flawlessly.

Phoenix Windshield Replacement Services

Using a fleet of mobile auto glass technicians, Universal Glass services the greater Maricopa County area with windshield chip repair, windshield replacement, rear window replacement and auto glass repair. All of our technicians have years of experience in replacing laminated auto glass in all vehicle types, years, makes and models. We only use the highest quality glass and sealants to ensure the safety and integrity of windshield replacements. We offer a lifetime warranty on all auto glass jobs and are approved by all insurance providers to perform a zero deductible windshield replacement or chip repair. Free auto glass repair quotes are available on our website or can be received over the phone by calling one of our auto glass replacement experts.

Window Repair & Glass Replacement Services

Our glaziers are capable of providing residential and light commercial glass replacements, window repairs, sliding glass door repairs and glass shower enclosure installations. Glass replacements in fixed, sliding and aluminum storefronts are available with either dual pane or single pane in a variety of glass types, colors and thicknesses. All of our glass is sourced from local manufacturers and comes with a warranty when a replacement is performed. Windows repairs include frame repair, roller replacement and window balance replacement. We can restore sliding glass doors to smoothly open and close.
