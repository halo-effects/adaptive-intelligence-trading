Natural Smiles

The fear of dental procedures keeps thousands of patients away from the care they need and deserve each year. Here at Natural Smiles, we are the family dentist committed to not only your dental health but your dental experience. We understand that some of our patients are a little apprehensive and that is why they often choose us for their care over anybody else in town. For years, we have provided dental care with a smile to Salem, Oregon and in return, restored the smiles of many happy patients who now love coming to the dentist. Relax at the Dentist? You Bet! Whether you are in need of an emergency dentist for a dental problem that just will not wait or a cosmetic dentist to put that shimmer back in your grin, we provide a relaxing dental experience that is both gentle, pain-free and laid back. We use both bedside manner and modern medicine to make your experience a pain-free appointment that you won't mind having again. Many of our patients fall asleep in the chair even without sedation. That is because we work hard to treat you like we would want to be treated. No surprises. No kidding. If you need extra numbing medication or are feeling a bit uneasy - raise your hand. We listen. Here at Natural Smiles, we want you to smile naturally. A natural smile comes from within and that is exactly what our team hopes to provide you with. We want you to feel joyful when you come to see us and we want to make sure that you always want to come back. Do not delay the treatment that you need due to a dental phobia. We work with anxious patients all the time and today... they are proudly coming in, grinning ear to ear, ready to see the dentist. So go ahead - say cheese! You're at Natural Smiles and it's time to get your smile back.
